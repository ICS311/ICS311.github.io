Which algorithm would you use?

Suppose you are faced with a computational problem and can think of two ways to solve it for an arbitrary number of n items.

Solution A
You first think of a solution that will be very easy to program. Once written, it will take 2n steps to solve the problem for n items.
Solution B
You realize another solution that is more difficult to write. Once written, it will take 100n2 steps to solve the problem for n

For what (integer) values of n will Solution A be faster, and for what values will Solution B be faster?

Lessons: For a small problem an easy solution may be OK, but be careful not to rely on estimates for small values: look at the overall growth of the function describing the solution's use of resources.
